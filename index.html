About Me

Hello there! My name is Daisy and I have a passion for seeking out innovative solutions through technology for everyday problems. I come from the world of healthcare and have seen first hand the impact technology can have in revolutionizing healthcare for both patients and healthcare teams. Recognizing the impact of this, I made the decision to to transition into software engineering to learn more about the underlying mechanisms behind the technology we interact with and to merge my creativity and skills set.

I also have a passion for promoting diversity and inclusion in all spaces. We all benefit from the richness of having diverse teams and ideas. The first step is recognizing the disparities for certain communities and how we can participate in closing those gaps. Please check out my "Diversifying Tech" section below for more resources related to diversity in tech.
